The 35th anniversary of

The Legend of Zelda

The time has come!

It's been a long time since february 1986 when the first copies of The legend of Zelda hit the stores (at least in Japan). Now, 35 years and several games later, we are glad to announce the series is still triving, and not just that, it's better than ever.

Let's review some of the announcements that have been made to celebrate!!.

A sequel to The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the wild

Since the E3 of 2019, most of us fans of TLOZ have been waiting for the sequel to this masterpiece. Now, in this year's E3 we had the chance to see much more about the sequel, which seem to have a lot of events in the skies (like in Skyward Sword), and has an official relase date for 2022.

Zelda themed Game & watch

Just like in Mario's 35th anniversary, Nintendo decided to create an special version of Game and watch console that allows to play the first to games of the series, as well as Link's awakening, y una versión de Vermin con Link como protagonista. Así como un reloj interactivo y muchos secretos!
